- 5k speech requests or speech intervals. ($20 value)
- 10k text requests. ($7.50 value)
**Paid Tier**: 
- $0.004 per speech request
- $0.00075 per text request.
- $0.0065 per 15-second speech interval.
- $0.50 per minute for chatbot training.
